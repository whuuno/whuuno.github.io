About Me

I am a Senior Software Engineer with 4+ years of experience designing and building scalable backend systems, distributed data platforms, and financial transaction infrastructure.
I work comfortably across:
  • Java, Spring Boot, REST APIs
  • Apache Spark, PySpark, Kafka
  • SQL & NoSQL Databases
  • Docker, Kubernetes, AWS
  • AI integrations

My core expertise lies in Java Spring Boot microservices, event-driven systems, and cloud-native architecture. I have worked extensively on high-throughput payment systems, secure wallet transactions (CBDC), regulatory APIs, and real-time reconciliation workflows — ensuring transactional integrity, consistency, and compliance. Alongside backend engineering, I specialize in data engineering — building Spark-based distributed pipelines that ingest data from Kafka, databases, and file systems to generate multi-country KPIs and analytics at scale.

About my Journey, I secured AIR 1092 in JEE Advanced and pursued B.Tech in Electronics & Communication Engineering at IIT Roorkee. My interest in Robotics led me to compete in Srishti (Annual Tech Fest) under the Models and Robotics Society, where my team secured 2nd position for building a Ball Balancing Platform. That experience sparked my curiosity in intelligent systems. As a core member of the Artificial Intelligence and Electronics Society (ArIES), I explored machine learning, neural networks, and deep learning. I implemented a CNN-based Facial Emotion Recognition system as part of the project "Ghost in Box".

In 2020, I worked as a Data Science Trainee at Envestnet Yodlee, where I developed models for detecting recurring transactions. Soon after, I transitioned into backend engineering — focusing deeply on distributed systems, event-driven architectures, and scalable microservices.
